About

ASHWINI DEY

I am professionally a Visual Effects Artist who loves to draw and click in his spare time, basically on weekends. I have always enjoyed Art from an early age. My earliest memory of this is doodling on A4 sheets dad bought for our printer. I can remember spending most of my childhood just drawing and being inspired to make anything and everything from household items to sceneries outside the window! This love of Art has stayed with me and developed and grown, and till now it is one thing that gives me peace of mind . I had always wanted to work within the Art and Design industry because it has always felt like the right direction for me to take. For as long as I can remember, I have wanted to go to an Art school but later on decided to combine my passion for art and movies and went for VFX Industry! I enjoy being creative and I view Art as a natural way for me to express myself. I particularly enjoy drawing and how this allows me to create images in a way that I see them. I enjoy working in a detailed way and I aim to make my work expressive and visually interesting.

When I joined the VFX industry, camera got to my hands and I started capturing moments that fascinated me. Now whenever I go out I have my camera with me. I see anything out of order or different or just beautiful, I capture it to freeze the time forever. My goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength, pain and enthusiasm of nature and people. I focus on non-models and place regular objects in exaggerated situations that highlight an aspect of sense and creativity. I look for a sense of defiance and fortitude in my work.
